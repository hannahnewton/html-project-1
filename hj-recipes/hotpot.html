hotpot recipe

Hotpot is a famous Chinese dish, Thinly sliced meats and vegetables are dipped in a hot broth seasoned with kelp and then swished back and forth several times (to cook) before being eaten

A big pot is set in the center of the table containing boiling hot broth with a heating element underneath. Various raw ingredients are served around the pot, such as thinly sliced meat, seafood, vegetables, tofu, dumplings, and noodles. The guests cook the ingredients in the broth and eat them with a dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • hotpot sauce
  • herbs such as mints, cilantro
  • preferred choice of meats
  • preferred choice of vegetables
  • dipping sauce

Steps

  1. mix preprepared sauce with boiling water
  2. set up cleaned meats and vegetables
  3. mix ingredients for dipping sauce
  4. boiling, dipping and eating
